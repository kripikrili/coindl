<p>This article provides a list of companies enabling you to purchase bitcoins using a bank transfer or a bank account. There are countries that offer transfer types that are specific like ACH transfer (USA) or SEPA transfer.</p>
 
<h2>Coinbase</h2>

<p>It is considered the largest bitcoin broker in the world. In United States, you can purchase bitcoin with a bank account connected through ACH bank transfer. In Europe, you can purchase as well via SEPA transfer using your bank account. Bitcoins bought with ACH will take five (5) days to be delivered; at the same time bitcoins bought via SEPA will takes two (2) days or less. </p>

<p>A flat 1.49% is charged as a fee for all transaction in Coinbase. Americans can opt to add a MasterCard or a Visa credit card to be used as a backup in order to unlock prompt payment through bank transfer.</p>
 
 
<strong>PROS</strong>
<ul>
<li>High liquidity as well as buying limits</li>
<li>It’s simple for newbies to get bitcoins</li>
<li>An available "Instant Buy" option with debit card or credit card</li>
</ul>
<strong>CONS</strong>
<ul>
<li>Bank transfer made buying can take as much as five ( 5) days to complete</li>
<li>Coinbase can track where and how your bitcoins are spent</li>
</ul>
